Our Story

Sandwedges is all ablout awesome sandwiches and happy people. By awesome sandwiches we mean wholesome yet unboring experience for you. Our sandwiches are made to order and generously filled with fresh goodness between artisan breads. We believe that’s the way sandwiches should be. Our story – How it all started.

A guy and a girl meets, and something between them clicks. To the world, they look poles apart. He is a backbencher, she prefers the first bench. He is a thinker, she is doer. He is a methodologist, she goes with the flow. But together, they know they can rock the world! It was a perfect weather afternoon (well, as perfect as it could get in Ghaziabad), and they were out for golgappas. The girl couldn’t stop talking about Mumbai and its famous street food, but the guy could think of just one thing. In a hasty moment, before he could procrastinate more, he blurted “Will you be my Co-founder?” And that’s how Sandwedges started (well, almost!) Arushi and Chaman met in IMT Ghaziabad and though both of them had more than once thought of starting up on their own, somehow things had not worked for them yet. But when her love for food and his love for business came together, there was no looking back. First store in RDC was launched just after they completed first year of B-school. And by the time they graduated, they were on a look out for the second store. It wasn’t easy, juggling classes, assignments and the outlet all at once, but if you were to ask them if it was worth the trouble, the answer is “Hell Yeah!!”

Plan Your Party

There is the 'Bai' who wont turn up on time, the neighbors who will pester you to lower the volume, and you would be too busy playing the host to actually enjoy the party! While we can’t help you sort out the neighbors or the 'bai', we definitely can sort out your enjoyment. You have already tried our lip-smacking sandwiches and burgers (psst..if you havn't tried them yet go, run, you are missing out on something!). However for the parties we bring a yummy army of Pastas, Salads and Desserts too!! Some of the parties catered by us are American Theme Party and Rain Dance Party. Our hostesses for these parties wanted to have a good time with their friends without having the hassle of cooking and dishes! So we arranged a spread for them after understanding their preferences. Not only did they enjoy, their friends loved the food! So sit back and relax, and let us be the hosts to your dosts!!
